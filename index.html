Books

The Road to OZ

by L. Frank Baum

Dorothy, Toto, Orang-orangan Sawah, Manusia Kayu Timah, dan Singa Pengecut kembali ke Negeri Oz setelah petualangan sebelumnya. Mereka bertemu dengan karakter baru, seperti Button-Bright dan Manusia Berbulu, yang memiliki kepala yang berubah-ubah. Dalam perjalanan mereka, mereka mengunjungi kota-kota seperti Foxville dan Dunkiton, di mana Button-Bright dan Manusia Berbulu mendapatkan kepala mereka kembali. Mereka juga menghadapi rintangan seperti gurun yang berbahaya dan makhluk jahat, seperti Scoodlers, sebelum akhirnya tiba di Kota Zamrud dan menghadiri pesta ulang tahun Putri Ozma.

A Child's History of England

by Charles Dickens

A Child's History of England first appeared in serial form, running from January 25, 1851 to December 10, 1853 and was first published in three volume book form in 1852, 1853, and 1854. Dickens dedicated the book to "My own dear children, whom I hope it may help, bye and bye, to read with interest larger and better books on the same subject". The history covered the period between 50 BC and 1689, ending with a chapter summarising events from then until the ascension of Queen Victoria.
